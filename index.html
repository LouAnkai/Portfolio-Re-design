Why do I want to learn web development?

April 6, 2019

The measure of intelligence is the ability to change. - Albert Einstein

With recent advancements in areas such as AI, big data and machine learning, the Internet has rapidly accelerated the rate at which it changes the way we live. In order to take part in this change, I need a better understanding of how these advancements use the Internet to offer users better solutions to life's problems. Since web developers are responsible for creating the front-end experience, I believe that web development is a good first step towards understanding how these solutions work.

My Background

April 6, 2019
